Module 6: Psychology of Money

Understand the psychological factors that influence financial decisions and learn strategies to overcome common biases.

Common Financial Biases
  • Loss Aversion: Fear of losing money often outweighs potential gains
  • Anchoring: Relying too heavily on the first piece of information
  • Confirmation Bias: Seeking information that confirms existing beliefs
  • Herd Mentality: Following the crowd without independent analysis
Overcoming Strategies
  • Automate Decisions: Set up automatic savings and investments
  • Diversify: Spread risk across different asset classes
  • Long-term Focus: Ignore short-term market noise
  • Regular Reviews: Periodic portfolio rebalancing
